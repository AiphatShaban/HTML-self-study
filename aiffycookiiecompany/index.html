Choco Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup of sugar2 teaspoons Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups of flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Walnuts or pecans, chopped (optional)
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Cacao Baking chips

Baking Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375'F
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar and
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time.
    Mix on low speed until incorporated
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture.
    Stir in nuts and chocolate
  6. Drop by teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown
  8. Enjoy!
